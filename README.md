"Rare Bird Hunt".
The game is about the adventurous ornithologistin search of record rare birds thou out the world which are repesented by airpots . 
Rules for the game is to manage fuel and resources and avoid dangers like storms or poachers.

-The main stages of the game:

1.Starting at the first airport.
2.Selecting the next airport to fly to.
3.Finding and recording rare birds.
4.Managing your fuel and other resources.
5.Returning to the starting airport to complete your mission.



-Key Features
As an player you can do :
1.Select and fly to different airports on your journey.
2.Record a rare bird to earn points.
3.Purchase equipment to make it easier to record birds.
4.Manage your fuel to ensure you don't run out mid-flight.
5.Return to the starting airport to complete the game.


-Rules 
• Airport and bird information should load quickly (under 2 seconds)
• The player should get instant feedback for every action
• The game should be stable and allow adding new airports or birds later
• The game should be suitable for players aged 12+ and contain no inappropriate content
